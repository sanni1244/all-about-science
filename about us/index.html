About Us

It is our mission to push the boundaries of science and technology to develop future physics, chemists, biologists and engineers that will help make the future a better place and uncover the truths of the world.

With our CEO, Dr. Fali Usman, we plan on making our facilities nation wide across Nigeria and then across Africa. We believe Africans have what it takes to uncover the beauty that the future entails. Let's make the leap together

Our organization is divided into 4 major departments, which are Biology, Physics, Chemistry and Engineering

Our major purpose is to train and mentor young scientists who have troubles making their ideas a reality or are have financial challenges with their experiments. In addition to that, we also train willing and commited students, but we assure that the training will be difficult and demanding so we advice that only those willing and ready should apply
